Preparation Tiem

  • Total:Aproximent 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Add fillings: Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the edges start to set.
  5. Fold and serve: If using cheese, sprinkle it on one half of the omelette.
  6. Enjoy: Using a spatula, fold the other half of the omelette over the cheese.
